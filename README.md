# Comprehensive Analysis of Fragrances in the E-Commerce Market

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive data-driven analysis of the 2024 fragrance e-commerce landscape. By analyzing 2,000 perfume listings from eBay, the study identifies meaningful patterns in pricing, brand dominance, and consumer behavior to support evidence-based business decisions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Data Analysis:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)
*   **Visualization:** Seaborn, Matplotlib
*   **Business Intelligence:** Power BI[cite: 1]

## 📊 Business Insights
*   **Gender Sales Gap:** Men’s fragrances are the primary engine of growth, moving nearly **0.8M units** compared to **0.5M units** for Women[cite: 1].
*   **The Price Ceiling:** Sales volume drops significantly once a product is priced above **$60**[cite: 1]. Most successful products are clustered in the **$20–$40** range[cite: 1].
*   **Market Share:** **Versace** is the top-performing brand, accounting for **26.17%** of all sales among the top 10 brands[cite: 1].
*   **Product Performance:** Eau de Parfum (EDP) listings command a higher price premium, but correct categorization is essential for turnover[cite: 1].

## 📂 Data Lifecycle & Processing
To ensure data integrity, the following preprocessing steps were performed:
1.  **Cleaning:** Handled missing values by imputing "Unknown" for brands and "0" for inactive sales/availability[cite: 1].
2.  **Standardization:** Uniformed fragrance types (e.g., converting "EDP" and "Eau de Perfume" to "Eau de Parfum")[cite: 1].
3.  **Outlier Removal:** Applied the **Interquartile Range (IQR)** method to filter extreme price points that could skew analysis[cite: 1].
4.  **Feature Engineering:** Derived gender labels and updated timeline features to reflect 2024 market data[cite: 1].

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
*   **Inventory:** Prioritize high-velocity brands like **Versace, Calvin Klein, and Davidoff**[cite: 1].
*   **Pricing:** To maximize profit through volume, keep listings within the **$25–$35 "Sweet Spot"**[cite: 1].
*   **Marketing:** Focus on Men's Gift Sets and repeat-purchase customers, as the men's segment moves **55% more volume**[cite: 1].
